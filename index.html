About

DJ K3YVE is a Brazilian DJ and music producer known for blending funk with modern electronic influences.

His sound explores energetic rhythms, heavy basslines and catchy melodies, creating tracks that feel raw. K3YVE is also a Cover-art designer, working specially for Rubix Records™ and Selected Artists, such as "DJ Javi26".

His arts travels around all the world, Being a inspiration to people and going through worldwide hits.
